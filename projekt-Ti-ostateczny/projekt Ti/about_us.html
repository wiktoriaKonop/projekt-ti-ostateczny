FAQ

Where can I find flight number?

You can find your flight number on elektronic ticket, which we send on your email.


Where can I check status of the flight?

You should write number of your flight on the status of the flight side.


How much is excess baggage?

The price depends on the route. It is different for everyone.


How can I return the ticket?

If you want to return a ticket, please fill out the contact form. Your application will be forwarded for processing in accordance with the ticket tariff conditions.


How can I check in for a flight?

The easiest way to check in is online check-in. Please note that due to various restrictions, some airports do not offer online check-in. In these cases, you must check in at the airport - it is free with our airlines.


Are there limits for transporting animals?

Yes, depending on the type of aircraft operating a given flight, there is a limited number of places for pets, so before buying a ticket, check whether there are still free places on a given flight.


Where can I check if I am entitled to an in-flight meal?

In our lines, we guarantee meals to every passenger during every journey
